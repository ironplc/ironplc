# SAGE: Skill Augmented GRPO Framework

## 🛰️ The SSOT for High-Reliability Industrial Intelligence

**SAGE (Skill Augmented GRPO)** is the authoritative intelligence framework governing the Sovridium™ Framework Automation approach. It is designed to bridge the gap between high-velocity AI synthesis and the "Rocket-Grade" integrity required for Industrial Control Systems (PLC, SCADA, etc.).

---

## 🏗️ Core Architecture

### 1. Skill Augmented (The "Atoms" of Logic)

SAGE rejects the "Black Box" approach to AI. Instead, it utilizes **Skills** as modular, auditable, and signed procedural units.

- **Skill Composition**: Each skill combines a clear **Intent** (The "What") and a **Method** (The formal logic or safety-critical algorithm).
- **Auditability**: Every action taken by the AI is traceable back to a validated Method. This eliminates the risk of hallucinations in safety-critical environments.
- **Bred for Resilience**: Skills are modular habits that are refined through iteration in the Proving Loop.

### 2. GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization)

GRPO is the mathematical engine that ensures the safest path is chosen through competitive selection.

#### Why GRPO? (The Contextual "Why")

- **Resource Efficiency**: GRPO deletes the "Critic" model found in traditional RL (like PPO). This allows the optimization loop to run on low-power **Industrial SBCs** and Edge Gateways, fulfilling the **E3 Endogenous** mandate.
- **Value of the Group Baseline**: Instead of comparing a move to an abstract ideal, GRPO compares it to a **Group** of 8–16 candidate solutions.
- **The Safest Path**: In a crisis where no "perfect" solution exists, GRPO identifies the **least catastrophic** path by calculating the relative advantage score within the group.
- **Kinetic Updates (75MPH Tire Change)**: SAGE is designed for **Bumpless Transfer**. It focuses on **Lock-Step Event/Signal Synchronization**, allowing new control logic to be "hot-reloaded" into a running factory process by precisely synchronizing state and signals before handover.
- **VCAT (Version Control & Audit Trail)**: Every logic change is governed by an immutable audit trail. SAGE ensures compliance with **FDA 21 CFR Part 11** and **GAMP 5** for electronic records, and **ISA-88** for batch control safety, providing a deterministic 'Flight Data Recorder' for all autonomous and human-attested actions.

## 🔄 The Proving Loop (Safety-First Synthesis)

The Proving Loop is the continuous cycle within the Sovridium Foundry modeled after **HazOp (Hazard and Operability)** and **Industrial Chemical Process** safety protocols:

1. **Anomaly Proposer**: Generates simulated industrial crises (sensor failures, hazardous leaks) in a digital twin.
2. **Logic Synthesis**: Agents compose sets of **Skills** (Intent + Method) to resolve the anomaly.
3. **Safety Calculus**: Candidate paths are verified against **Rocket-Grade Flight Safety** standards for software-defined automation.
4. **GRPO Optimization**: Paths with the highest safety-margin advantage over the group average are reinforced.
